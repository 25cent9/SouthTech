Healthy Living Plan

Many people today are trying to stay healthy. What sorts of things do you need to do in order to maintain a healthy body? Here are some ideas for you. Of course, you should always consult with your doctor before undertaking any new diet or exercise plan.

Eat Right

Refer to the United States Department of Agriculture food pyramid to find out how many grains, vegetables, fruits, meats, dairy, and oils that you should eat daily. Also consult the other resources avaliable on the Internet to determine a plan that will work for you.

Keep a food, mood, and exercise journal

  • Track what you eat each day
  • Track your mood throughout the day
  • Track the exercise that you do each day
