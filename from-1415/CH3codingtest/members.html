Membership Options

Pick up form at the Club or email Stacy Hicks to request one.

Individual memberships(must be 18)

Day-time: This low-cost option allows you full access to the Club from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Saturday.

All hours: This option allows you full access to the Club during all hours.

Family memberships (for immediate family only)

With child care: This option is the best for families who plan to use the children's play room. The play room is for children 6 weeks to 12 years. There are no extra fees for use of the play room, and it's open every day.

Without child care: Save a little on your membership if your family won't need acces to the play room.

Punch cards (try the Club or give a healthy gift)

Our punch cards give guests full access to the Club (except for the play room) for an entire day. Punch cards are available for 5, 10, and 20 visits.
