Fight Against World Hunger

Our school and schools around the world have recently joined forces to fight against world hunger. The office for the Fight Against Hunger team is located next to the bookstore. You can drop off food and money donations there. We also have donation drop-off locations at participating grocery stored. See the list of stores as well as additional information on the bulletin board outside our team office.

What you can do to help:

  • Join the schools around the world to Fight Against World Hunger with a $10 donation
  • Buy requested food at participating grocery stores and leave at drop-off locations
  • Volunteer to collect money and donations around the community
