Instructions

Use the "Start" button to start spinning and the "Stop" button to stop spinning. The point system is as followed.
  • Horizontal: 10 Points
  • Diagonal: 20 Points
  • Free space randomly appears on board every four turns
    • 2 matching and a free space, horizontal: 10 points
    • 2 matching and a free space, diagonal: 20 points
